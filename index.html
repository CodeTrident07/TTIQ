◎ The Story
I started playing table tennis at 13 years old. By 15 I was competing for Shabab Al Ahli at the U15 level, and went on to represent the club at U17 and U19. I played competitively from 2022 to 2024, and again from 2025 to 2026, picking up multiple awards both in club competitions and individual tournaments.

But throughout all of it, something nagged at me. Even when my technique was sharp and my fitness was good, I'd lose points I shouldn't — not because of physical failure, but because of tactical and mental gaps. I'd make the wrong decision under pressure. I'd miss patterns in my opponent's game. I'd tighten up at 9-10 in the fifth.

No app existed that addressed this properly for players at my level. So I built one.

✦ What TT IQ Fixes
TT IQ is built around the gap between physical ability and match performance. The tools inside target exactly what I was missing:

Match Logger — turns every rally into data so patterns become visible, not just felt.
Analytics — surfaces your real weaknesses, not the ones you assume you have.
Tactical Quiz — trains your in-match decision-making so the right choice becomes instinctive.
Drill Generator — converts identified weaknesses into structured, purposeful practice sessions.

This is the platform I wish existed when I was 15. It's for anyone who takes the game seriously.
